Tropical Fruits 🍍

Mango Pineapple Papaya Coconut Banana Guava Lychee Passion Fruit Durian Star Fruit

Mango 🍋

Description: Known as the "king of fruits," mangoes are sweet, juicy, and have a smooth texture. They are rich in vitamins A and C and are often eaten raw, in smoothies, or used in cooking.

Health Benefits: Boosts immunity, improves digestion, and is great for skin health.

Pineapple 🍍

Description:The taste of a pineapple tends to be both sweet and piquant. The meat contains vitamin C, and its bright yellow color adds to its appeal. They can be eaten raw or in an array of beverages, sweets, and even main courses.

Health Benefits:High in vitamin C, bromelain (an omnigredient) which aids digestion, and has anti inflammation benefits.

Papaya 🍈

Description: Papayas are soft, tropical fruits with an orange color and sweet, musky flavor. The flesh is rich in enzymes like papain, which helps with digestion.

Health Benefits: Excellent for digestive health, boosts the immune system, and is rich in antioxidants.

Coconut 🥥

Description:Coconuts are an all-in-one package as they provide coconut water (an excellent source of hydration and electrolytes), coconut meat (protein-rich with healthy fats), and even coconut milk/oil.

Health Benefits: Hydrates the body, supports heart health, and is great for skin and hair care.

Banana 🍌

Description: Bananas are easy-to-peel fruits that are energy-rich and a great source of potassium. They are often eaten as a snack or added to smoothies and desserts.

Health Benefits: Provides quick energy, aids in digestion, and helps balance blood pressure.

Guava 🥭

Description: Guavas are small, round, and sweet-tart fruits with a green or yellow skin. They are rich in dietary fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants.

Health Benefits: Supports digestion, boosts immunity, and improves heart health.

Lychee 🍇

Description: Lychees are small, round fruits with a bumpy red skin and translucent, juicy flesh inside. They are sweet, fragrant, and are often used in desserts or eaten fresh.

Health Benefits: Rich in vitamin C, antioxidants, and supports skin health.

Passion Fruit 🥝

Description: Passion fruit has a tough outer rind with a juicy, seed-filled interior. The flavor is tangy and sweet, and it is often used in drinks or desserts.

Health Benefits: Aids digestion, helps reduce stress, and supports heart health.

Durian 🥥

Description: Known as the "king of fruits" in Southeast Asia, durians have a strong odor but a sweet, creamy interior. It’s an acquired taste.

Health Benefits: Rich in fiber, vitamin C, and antioxidants.

Star Fruit ⭐

Description: Star fruit has a unique star-shaped cross-section. It’s tangy and sweet, often used in salads or eaten fresh.

Health Benefits: Low in calories, high in vitamin C, and helps in hydration.
